About the Artist

Crafting Electronic Symphonies

With over 8 years in the electronic music scene, Alex Rodriguez has become a defining voice in progressive house and techno. From underground clubs to major festivals, his unique sound has captivated audiences worldwide.

His journey began in the vibrant nightlife of Miami, where he honed his skills mixing tracks that seamlessly blend deep basslines with ethereal melodies. Today, he's recognized for his innovative approach to electronic music production.
